# FaceRecogntion-attandance-system
The system's primary concept boils down to recognizing students by their faces and marking them as attendants. By video-capturing, the lecture hall, our technology recognizes several faces in real-time. Face detection is the first stage after recording live footage. After face recognition, the system scans the database for every face match, producing an excel sheet with the students' names and other information the instructor need. The next step was to extract embeddings from the photos using a pre-trained OpenFace model after we completed the face identification section with OpenCV and deep learning.
